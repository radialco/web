About

Radialco specializes in providing conceptual design, CAD, animation, and rendering services geared toward communicating ideas for aerospace projects. We develop concepts for spacecraft, habitats, modules, space stations, rockets, monolithic satellites, cubesats, and even individual parts for the aerospace industry, with a focus on multimedia digital previsualization.







FAQs



Can we work together?

Yes, of course; we make all our knowledge available to develop new projects. Send an E-Mail or contact vía Fiverr



My project is still in the development phase, what is needed to start developing a preview?

No problem; it's important to communicate the ideas behind each project, whether to make it known within a community or to attract investors. The most important thing to start creating a preview is a brief description of the idea or concept and, if necessary, some sketches, scale comparisons, and references.



Do you work under confidentiality agreements (NDA)?

Yes! In fact, we recommend clients draft their own confidentiality agreement if they wish and it makes them feel more secure. In any case, commissions are based on the idea of ​​confidentiality from the outset and are only published upon explicit agreement between both parties, for illustrative purposes only. Commissions under NDA do not have any additional cost.
